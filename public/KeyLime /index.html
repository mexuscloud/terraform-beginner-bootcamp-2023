Key Lime Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup key lime juice (freshly squeezed if possible)
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom and up the sides of a pie dish to form the crust.
  4. Bake the crust for 8-10 minutes until lightly golden. Remove and let it cool.
  5. In another bowl, whisk the egg yolks until slightly thickened.
  6. Add the condensed milk and whisk until well combined.
  7. Gradually whisk in the key lime juice and lime zest until the mixture thickens slightly.
  8. Pour the filling into the cooled crust.
  9. Bake the pie for 10-12 minutes until the filling is set but not browned.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
  11. Serve chilled, optionally topped with whipped cream.

Enjoy this delightful and tangy Key Lime Pie!
